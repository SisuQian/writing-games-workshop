简介 Introduction

乌力波（法语：Ouvroir de littérature potentielle, Oulipo）意为"潜在文学工场"，于1960年在巴黎创立，旨在通过人为限制探查语言的潜在结构。它将文学从灵感中心转向可设计、可生成的模式实验。20世纪60—70年代，随着乔治·佩雷克等成员的加入，乌力波系统化发展出多种写作方法，并与结构主义的语言观形成共振，强调文本由结构生成而非单纯表达。1990年代起，乌力波在数字人文与算法写作领域获得新解释，被视为生成式文本与程序性写作的早期模型，其影响跨越文学、数学、游戏研究与概念艺术。

Oulipo (Ouvroir de littérature potentielle), founded in Paris in 1960, explores the latent structures of language through deliberately designed constraints. It shifts writing away from inspiration-as-origin toward reproducible, generative experiments. With members such as Georges Perec, Oulipo developed a systematic repertoire of methods resonating with structuralist views of text as produced by form. Since the 1990s, it has been re-read through digital humanities and algorithmic writing—as an early model for procedural and generative text across literature, mathematics, game studies, and conceptual art.

理念 Concept

写作不仅是表达工具，也是可以被设计、被触发、被生成的实践。
本工作坊把"游戏"当作通道：结构先行，让语言在规则中自行生长。

Writing is not only expression but a practice that can be designed, triggered, and generated.
Here, constraints function as a passage: structure leads, and language grows inside rules—through collective reading and iterative feedback.

规则 Rules 生成 Generation 结构 Structure 共同阅读 Collective Reading 互评 Iterative Feedback
方法 Method

在「SISU乌力波写作游戏工作坊」中，乌力波作为即兴创作的通道，以游戏介入写作，在跨越方法与多语境下体验乌力波的创作乐趣。愿每位参与者沉浸其中，有所收获。

Within the SISU Oulipo Writing Games Workshop, Oulipo works as a channel for improvisation. We approach writing through play, testing constraints across contexts and methods. Participants move between exercises, observation/notation, discussion, and rewriting—customizable to the venue and audience.

短规则写作 Constrained Writing 观察/记录 Observation · Notation 讨论与改写 Discussion · Rewriting

工作室简介 About SISU Studio

SISU Studio 成立于 2016 年，是一间创意驱动型艺术工作室，为创作者及机构提供项目策划、创意支持与专业服务。工作室与教育机构、文化项目、展览馆及商业公司开展合作，参与近百场展演及文化项目的策划与落地，实践覆盖中国各地。近年来，工作室聚焦人文跨学科实践，持续探索艺术、技术与商业融合的多种可能。

SISU Studio was founded in 2016 as a creativity-driven art studio. It provides project planning, creative support, and professional services for artists, creators, and institutions. The studio has collaborated with educational institutions, cultural projects, exhibition venues, and commercial companies, contributing to the planning and delivery of nearly one hundred exhibitions, performances, and cultural initiatives across multiple cities in China. In recent years, SISU Studio has focused on interdisciplinary practice, exploring new possibilities across art, technology, and commerce.
